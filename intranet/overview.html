Ecosystem Overview

An executive summary of Chandigarh University's student-led administrative networks, compliance systems, and operations.

Chandigarh University is a leading Indian institution committed to academic excellence, innovative research, and holistic student development. Recognized with an elite NAAC A+ grade, CU provides a thriving learning environment equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and mentored by distinguished faculty.

The Office of Academic Affairs (OAA) oversees a massive ecosystem of student-run co-curricular groups. This intranet portal serves as the central hub of information and administration for all clubs, communities, departmental societies, and professional societies across campus.

Co-Curricular Diversity

Over 80+ student entities specializing in coding, design, social impact, entrepreneurship, photography, debate, and fine arts.

Holistic Development

Programs designed to instil practical skills, leadership traits, and team-building capabilities, ensuring student readiness for global careers.

Empowering students to lead with confidence, responsibility, and vision under Chandigarh University's Office of Academic Affairs.
