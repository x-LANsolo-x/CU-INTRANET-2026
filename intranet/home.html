Dear Student,

Heartiest congratulations to you on joining Chandigarh University, a globally trusted and respected institution. As you embark on a new phase of life with us, you become part of a vibrant learning ecosystem that believes in your potential, nurtures your aspirations, and provides transformative opportunities and experiences to help you grow and contribute to society.

At Chandigarh University, students are at the heart of our vision and mission. Our student-centric approach is reflected in a multicultural community that brings together talented learners from all 28 states and 8 Union Territories of India, along with students from 68 countries, enriching your learning journey and preparing you to thrive in an interconnected world.

Our unwavering commitment to academic excellence, pioneering research, and global engagement has earned us the recognition of being ranked India’s No. 1 Private University for three consecutive years in the QS Asia University Rankings. Placed among the Top 1% of universities in India and the Top 2% of universities worldwide, along with a global rank of 526 in the coveted QS World University Rankings 2027, Chandigarh University empowers students across diverse disciplines through industry-aligned education guided by accomplished faculty and a strong network of more than 980 industry leaders.

The experiential pedagogy here, enriched by collaborations with over 530 institutions across more than 100 countries, creates avenues to learn by solving real-world challenges, engaging with diverse perspectives, and experiencing new cultures. Through international exchange programs, international internships, and strong corporate partnerships, you will acquire the knowledge, skills, and perspective to collaborate across cultures and build a truly global career.

Integral to this educational journey is a dynamic ecosystem of research and innovation that distinguishes Chandigarh University. With more than 30 industry-collaborated Centres of Excellence and Advanced Laboratories and 250 international partnerships spanning 100 nations for collaborative research, the University has established itself as India's Leading Research-Intensive Institution.

Whether your aspirations lie in entrepreneurship, sports, cultural pursuits, or community engagement, you will find an enabling environment and world-class infrastructure that nurtures talent and celebrates excellence. Through the Government of India-supported Technology Business Incubator (TBI), aspiring entrepreneurs receive mentorship, incubation support, funding opportunities, and access to industry stalwarts to transform their innovative ideas into successful ventures.

May your time with us enhance your intellect, strengthen your character, and inspire accomplishments worthy of your highest potential, which remain a source of pride throughout your life.

Once again, congratulations and welcome to the Chandigarh University family. I wish you an inspiring, fulfilling, and rewarding experience ahead.

Best Wishes,

Dr. Raviraja N. Seetharam

Vice Chancellor

FAQs

Everything You Need to Know

Here are solutions to the most common queries raised by freshmen during welcome week registration.

Is Welcome Week attendance mandatory?
Yes, all induction events, documentation clearances, syllabus walkthroughs, and VC welcoming plenaries are crucial and mandatory for every fresher student.
Where do I report on campus on the first day?
Refreshments and entry support desks will guide you from the main gate to the designated sports complex / auditorium based on your chosen program.
How can my parents reach campus? Are there transit guides?
Shuttle buses will make regular loops from major transit nodes. Local cabs are also available. Please refer to our shuttle schedule under our navigation services.
Can parents attend the orientation sessions?
Absolutely. Chandigarh University warmly welcomes parents. Special dining lounges and parent-interaction seminars are scheduled in academic blocks.
Who should I contact in case of delays or emergencies?
Please reach out to our dedicated university hotline at 1800 257 1800. Help desks are also operating inside all main campus entry checkpoints.
