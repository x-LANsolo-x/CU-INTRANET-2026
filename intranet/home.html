FAQs

Everything You Need to Know

Common questions from new students about joining communities, registering interest, and making the most of CU's co-curricular ecosystem.

How do I join a club or community at CU?
Fill in the Interest Registration form on this site. Club coordinators will review your preferences and reach out with induction details, audition schedules, or direct onboarding steps depending on the community.
Can I join multiple communities simultaneously?
Yes! Many students are active members of 2–3 clubs. You can express interest in multiple communities from a single registration form, and coordinators will help you manage scheduling conflicts.
Are there any eligibility criteria to join a club?
Most clubs are open to all students regardless of branch or year. Some performance-based communities (e.g., dance troupes, debate teams) may hold auditions or selection rounds, details of which are shared post-registration.
Do co-curricular activities count toward my academic credits?
Yes. CU's credit framework includes activity points for verified co-curricular participation. Your club coordinators and the OAA office can guide you on how to log hours and earn recognition credits.
Who do I contact if I have questions about the ecosystem?
Reach out to the Office of Academic Affairs (OAA) at admission@cumail.in or call our helpline at 1800 257 1800. You can also visit the OAA desk located at the main academic block during working hours.
