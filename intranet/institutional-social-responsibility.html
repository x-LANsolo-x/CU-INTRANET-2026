Social Responsibility

Empowering student engagement in rural development, ecological campaigns, and social welfare drives.

Community Awareness

Chandigarh University is dedicated to addressing developmental challenges in society. Through our Institutional Social Responsibility (ISR) initiatives, we instil social awareness and community empathy in our student community.

Community Empowerment

CU has adopted several surrounding villages, conducting regular health checkups, literacy drives, and providing computer training to local youth.

Environmental Drives

Active tree-plantation drives, paper recycling initiatives, zero-plastic zones, and student campaigns to clean public water streams.

Empowering students to lead with confidence, responsibility, and vision under Chandigarh University's Office of Academic Affairs.
