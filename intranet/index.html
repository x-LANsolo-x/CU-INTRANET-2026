Welcome to the Co-Curricular Ecosystem of Chandigarh University — a thriving network of 80+ officially recognized student entities operating under the Office of Academic Affairs (OAA). Vision & Mission

Our Purpose & Direction

Vision

To cultivate an inclusive, innovative, and globally recognized co-curricular ecosystem that empowers every student to become a competent leader, responsible citizen, lifelong learner, and industry-ready professional.

Mission

  • Foster holistic student development beyond academics.
  • Promote leadership, innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship.
  • Provide experiential learning through structured co-curricular engagement.
  • Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration and industry partnerships.
  • Build socially responsible and ethically driven future leaders.
  • Create opportunities that prepare students for global careers and lifelong success.
Our Foundation

What Powers Our Ecosystem

Every entity under the Office of Academic Affairs operates through four foundational pillars that ensure meaningful student engagement and measurable impact.

Student-Led Governance

Students lead their own organizations by planning events, managing operations, coordinating teams, and making strategic decisions while developing real-world leadership and management skills.

Faculty Mentorship

Dedicated Faculty Champions and Advisors provide strategic guidance, academic support, quality assurance, and mentorship to ensure sustainable growth and excellence.

Experiential Learning

Structured annual calendars include workshops, hackathons, competitions, research initiatives, community outreach, innovation challenges, and industrial interactions that bridge theory with practice.

Recognition & Achievement

Students receive official certifications, leadership appointments, awards, trophies, co-curricular credits, and institutional recognition that enhance both academic and professional portfolios.
