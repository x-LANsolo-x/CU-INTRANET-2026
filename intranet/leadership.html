University Leadership

The guiding perspective and leadership messages supporting extra-curricular excellence and student empowerment at Chandigarh University.

Chancellor's Message

"Chandigarh University is built on the foundation of student empowerment. We believe that true learning transcends classroom boundaries. Our co-curricular entities are designed to shape well-rounded, socially aware global professionals."

Vice-Chancellor's Message

"Integrity, innovation, and engagement form the core of our educational model. The Office of Academic Affairs (OAA) ensures that every student co-curricular group receives the resources, mentorship, and platform to scale their potential."

Empowering students to lead with confidence, responsibility, and vision under Chandigarh University's Office of Academic Affairs.
