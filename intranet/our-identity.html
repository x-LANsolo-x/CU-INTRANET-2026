Our Identity

Nurturing a diverse, global, and highly entrepreneurial community that learns beyond conventional boundaries.

Chandigarh University is characterized by its dynamic, cosmopolitan campus representing all 28 Indian States, 8 Union Territories, and over 50 nations. Our identity is rooted in fostering cross-cultural harmony, academic integrity, and an entrepreneurial mindset.

At CU, we believe that education extends far beyond textbooks. We empower students by giving them the leadership and autonomy to establish and run specialized entities, manage national-level festivals, and design real-world engineering prototypes.

Global Diversity

A vibrant melting pot of cultures, promoting global citizenship and mutual respect.

Innovation Spirit

Encouraging student patents, startup incubation, and cutting-edge research projects.

Leadership Culture

Providing student coordinators complete freedom to organize and lead large groups.

Empowering students to lead with confidence, responsibility, and vision under Chandigarh University's Office of Academic Affairs.
